DOJ:RP Sandy Shores

About the Game

DOJ:RP Sandy Shores is one of the best law enforcement and civilian roleplay experiences. Set in a decently sized Sandy Shores remade map, players can choose from multiple career paths including law enforcement, emergency services, civilians, and more.

Key Features

  • Semi-realistic law enforcement mechanics
  • Comprehensive civilian career options
  • Dynamic, player-driven narratives
  • Semi-active staff team
